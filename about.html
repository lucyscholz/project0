Hi, I'm Lucy!

I love creative problem-solving, community building, spending time outside, and learning new things.

Right now, I spend most of my time working in data and analytics for Collegiate Academies. I'm build dashboards, spreadsheets, and other data tools to help wrangling data so our schools and team can better serve our students.

When I'm not on my computer, I love spending time outside - especially in the summer. Since 2015, I've been a part of the founding team at Live Oak Wilderness Camp. We work to bring together amazing young leaders from all across New Orleans through fun, friendship, and leadership development. It's not just a summer camp, but rather a year-around leadership development program and community.

On the Live Oak team, I've...
  • Designed leadership development programming for young people, ages 7-18.
  • Created and managed our application process for over 300 campers per summer.
  • Created and managed our hiring process for about 60 staff per summer.
  • Designed and executed our staff training.
  • Developed our organization's strategic plan to expand our support.

When I'm not working for Collegiate Academies or Live Oak Wilderness Camp, I love running, cooking, learning new things, and playing games.
